Bihar Krishi – Agriculture Support App for Farmers in Bihar

Bihar Krishi is a government-backed app developed by the Agriculture Dept. of Bihar as part of the 4th Agriculture Roadmap initiative.

I played a key role in developing this cross-platform mobile application using Flutter, ensuring an intuitive user experience and accessibility even in low-connectivity areas. The app is available in local languages and is tailored specifically to the needs of the farming community.

Features: Scheme Information, Application Tracking, Multilingual Support, Push Notifications.

Impact

  • Empowered thousands of farmers across Bihar with crucial agricultural information with 100k+ downloads on playstore.
  • Improved participation in government schemes.
  • Strengthened digital inclusion in rural communities.

Spastik-App – Post-Stroke Spasticity Self-Monitoring Tool

Spastik-App is a mobile health application designed to empower stroke patients in the early detection of spasticity through regular self-monitoring. By using a structured questionnaire based on the traffic light principle, the app helps users identify early signs of complications and encourages timely medical consultation.

I was responsible for the mobile development using Flutter, ensuring that the app was accessible, responsive, and easy to use for patients of all ages and abilities.

Features: Medical Guidance, Reminder Notifications, Patient-Centric UX.

Impact

  • Supported early detection of post-stroke spasticity through a simple self-check approach.
  • Reduced potential complications by promoting timely contact with healthcare providers.
